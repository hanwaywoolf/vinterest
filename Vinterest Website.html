WineDNA

Your taste, finally understood.

Most people drink what they know because they don't know what they like. WineDNA changes that. Every bottle you scan adds another layer to a portrait of your palate — your favourite regions, your go-to grapes, your body preferences, your flavour signatures — built entirely around you.

It goes deep — Not just what you like, but why. WineDNA explains your taste in plain language — so you understand yourself as a wine drinker, not just what's in your glass.

It evolves with you — Your palate isn't static and neither is your WineDNA. Scan more, explore more, and watch your taste profile sharpen with every pour.

It covers everything — Separate profiles for reds, whites, rosé, and sparkling — because your taste in a Burgundy and your taste in a Blanc de Blancs are two very different conversations.

Sommelier Script

Always know what to say.

Most people freeze when a sommelier asks what they like. Or they point at a price point and hope for the best. Your Sommelier Script changes that — a personalised, WineDNA-powered description of your taste, in your own words, ready whenever you need it.

Short enough to say at the table. Long enough to impress at the cellar door. Separate scripts for reds, whites, rosé, and sparkling — so whatever you're ordering, you sound exactly like someone who knows their wine.
